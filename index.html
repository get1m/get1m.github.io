1m Coin Price Alert

1m is the new price checker app for Bitcoin, Ethereum and other altcoins with intuitive, clean interface that helps you stay one step ahead of the market at all times. With smart alarms and clean UI designed to let you quickly and effortlessly manage your alerts, you can easily stay up to date with the market, even when you are on the go or busy.

Cryptocurrency Price Tracker
Track prices of all your coins in real time using a clean UI to get a quick overview whenever you need it. You can simultaneously track different coins across different exchanges. Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other altcoins are supported!

Coin Price Alerts
Probably the most intuitive yet powerful coin price alarm app for Android. Setup alarms for your coins with just a few taps! The app gives you price level recommendations based on real time price. Keep your alarms smart!

Real Time Notifications
Stay in the loop with clean, simple and informative notifications designed to keep you informed while not being annoying.

100% free, no ads, no tracking, no account or signup required
This app is designed to be clean, simple and trustworthy. No special permissions required. Unlike some other similar apps, there is no spying or stealing your private information. Instead, 1m will offer an optional premium subscription in the near future in order to stay profitable.
